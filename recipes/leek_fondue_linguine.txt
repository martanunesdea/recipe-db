Leek fondue linguine
Ingredients
4 large leeks, whites and pale green parts shredded
50g butter
60ml white wine
6 tbsp crème fraîche
400g linguine
(or veggie alternative) shavings for the top parmesan or grana padano
½ lemon
Instructions
1. Put the leeks with half of the butter in a pan and melt the butter around the leeks. Add the remaining butter and white wine and cook very gently for 5 minutes. Add the crème fraîche and cook for another 15 minutes, or until the leeks are meltingly soft. Season well.
